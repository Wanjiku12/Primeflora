About us

Location: Our flower farm is nestled in Nakuru, Kenya, renowned for its ideal climate and rich soil, perfect for growing a wide variety of premium flowers. Our company was founded in 2015 by Mr. Moses Macharia, driven by a passion for floriculture and a commitment to sustainability. With over 9 years of dedicated cultivation, we have been producing high-quality flowers while adhering to eco-friendly farming practices.
