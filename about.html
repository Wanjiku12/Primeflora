About Us

Why PrimeFlora

Delivery

Same-Day Airport Shipment: Flowers are harvested in the morning and shipped the same day to preserve freshness. 48-Hour Shipment Window: Orders are shipped within 24 hours of harvest to maintain peak freshness. Careful Packaging: Flowers are sleeved and packaged with premium materials to protect blooms and maintain optimal conditions during transit. Efficient Logistics: Our team coordinates fast, reliable routes, closely monitoring shipments to prevent delays. Global Reach: With expertise in the EU market, we meet specific global standards. Quality Control: Strict measures ensure flowers arrive in pristine condition.

About us

Location: Our flower farm is nestled in Nakuru, Kenya, renowned for its ideal climate and rich soil, perfect for growing a wide variety of premium flowers. Our company was founded in 2015 by Mr. Moses Macharia, driven by a passion for floriculture and a commitment to sustainability. With over 9 years of dedicated cultivation, we have been producing high-quality flowers while adhering to eco-friendly farming practices.
